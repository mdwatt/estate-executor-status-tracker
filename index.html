How It Works

1
Upload the Estate File
"Attorney Creates the Case" — In under two minutes, then after Attorney creates the case, the Executor receives a secure magic link by email. No portal setup. No account creation.
2
Organize & Track Status
"Executor Uploads on Their Own" — The Executor follows the link, uploads each required document against the checklist, and the Attorney sees status update in real time. No follow-up calls needed.
3
Attorney Reviews and Closes
"Attorney" — Once all items are submitted, the Attorney reviews, marks each step complete, and closes the case with a full documented record.
