How It Works

1
Attorney Creates the Case
In under two minutes the Attorney creates the case. The Executor immediately receives a secure magic link by email. No portal setup. No account creation.
2
Executor Uploads on Their Own
The Executor follows the link and uploads each required document against the checklist. The Attorney sees status update in real time. No follow-up calls needed.
3
Attorney Reviews & Closes
Once all items are submitted, the Attorney reviews, marks each step complete, and closes the case with a full documented record.

Product Screens

Screen 1 — Executor Landing (Magic Link): The executor receives a secure link by email and lands here. Each of the 12 required estate steps is listed with its current status. Steps marked Not Started show an Upload button. Completed steps show a View button.

Click image to enlarge

Screen 2 — Upload Document: When the executor taps a step, this upload screen appears. It accepts PDF, JPG, or PNG up to 25MB. For electronically originated wills, a chain-of-custody checkbox captures timestamp, source IP, and SHA-256 checksum as evidentiary metadata.

Click image to enlarge

Screen 3 — Attorney View (Single Case): The attorney's dashboard for one case. Displays a live status summary, Step 11 soft deadline (AOC-E-505, 80 days from qualification), document review actions (Mark Reviewed / Reject), internal notes, and a one-click Export to PDF. The Resend Magic Link button re-sends the executor link if the 30-day link expires.
